ACME Corp — Employee Handbook (excerpt)

Vacation. Full-time employees receive 20 paid vacation days per year, accrued monthly. Unused days roll over up to a maximum of 5 days into the following year.

Requesting time off. Submit vacation requests through the HR portal at least two weeks in advance. Your manager must approve the request before the dates are confirmed.

Remote work. Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Fully remote arrangements require VP approval.

Expenses. Reimbursable expenses must be submitted within 30 days, each with a receipt. The monthly limit without pre-approval is 500 dollars.

Sick leave. Employees accrue 10 paid sick days per year, separate from vacation.
